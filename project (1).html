#Do charges of people who smoke differ significantly from the people who dont?

from scipy.stats import f_oneway
smoker=insurancedata[insurancedata['smoker']=='yes']
insurancedata_smoker_charges=smoker['charges']
non_smoker=insurancedata[insurancedata['smoker']=='no']
insurancedata_non_smoker_charges=non_smoker['charges']
f_oneway(insurancedata_smoker_charges,insurancedata_non_smoker_charges)
Out[142]:
F_onewayResult(statistic=2177.614868056519, pvalue=8.271435842182967e-283)

Since the P value is very low, there is significant differenece between the charges of people who smoke and who dont smoke.

We Reject the NULL hypothesis , since there is significat difference between the charges of people who smoke and who dont smoke.
